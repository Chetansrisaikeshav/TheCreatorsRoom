Our Mission

Bringing Telugu Stories to Life

lovetalkies delivers Telugu heart-touching stories, family drama, love, and feel-good stories through audio podcasts. With 5K+ subscribers and 383+ episodes, we've built a community that craves emotional storytelling.

Our Story

From Passion
to Platform

What started as a love for Telugu storytelling has grown into lovetalkies - a channel dedicated to bringing emotional, heart-touching narratives to listeners everywhere.

Our signature series PRANAYAM and PRIYASAKHI have captivated thousands, and now The Creators Room opens our doors for YOUR stories. Join us in building the largest Telugu audio story library.
